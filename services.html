Frequently Asked Questions

🎁

Everything you need to know about Cancan's Corner

Drop-off & Pickup
How does the drop-off process work?
+

It's simple! Schedule your drop-off through the booking link on the Contact page, then bring your unwrapped gifts during your chosen time slot. I'll handle everything from there and reach out as soon as they're ready for pickup.

How far in advance should I drop off my gifts?
+

I recommend at least 24–48 hours before you need them — especially around the holidays. If you're in a time crunch, reach out and I'll do my best to make it work!

When will my gifts be ready for pickup?
+

Most standard wrapping is done within 24 hours of drop-off. For larger or custom orders I'll give you an estimated time when you book. You'll hear from me as soon as everything's wrapped and ready!

Pricing & Payment
How much does gift wrapping cost?
+

Standard wrapping starts at $3–$8, Special Occasion packages start at $10, and Large or Custom orders are quoted individually. Check out the pricing cards above for a full breakdown of what's included in each tier!

How do I pay?
+

Payment is accepted via Cash App — just send to $Cancans92. Payment is due at or before pickup. Have questions? Send me a message through the Contact page anytime.

Do you charge extra for oddly shaped or large gifts?
+

Unusually large or tricky shapes typically fall under the Large/Custom quote tier. Just reach out before your drop-off and describe the item — I'll give you a price upfront so there are no surprises.

Supplies & Style
Do I need to bring my own wrapping paper or supplies?
+

Nope — supplies are included! If you have a specific paper, ribbon, or theme in mind you're welcome to bring your own and I'll work with it. Just mention it when you book so I can plan ahead.

Can I request a specific color, theme, or style?
+

Absolutely! There's a notes field in both the quote request and booking forms — share your occasion, colors, vibe, or anything else. The more detail you give me, the more personal I can make it.

Booking & Contact
What are your hours?
+

Drop-offs are available 7 days a week, 3 PM to 8 PM. You can book your slot anytime through the Calendly scheduler on the Contact page — it'll show you exactly what's open.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?
+

Life happens! Reschedule through the Calendly link in your booking confirmation email, or send me a message on the Contact page and I'll get you sorted out right away.
